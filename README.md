## 📖 About Retinasense-AI

RetinaSense AI is a software tool that uses artificial intelligence to examine pictures of your eye's retina. It looks closely at these images to spot signs of eye diseases early. The goal is to help eye doctors make faster and more accurate diagnoses. This means better care for your eyes and catching issues before they become serious.

The software works by analyzing fundus images—these are photos of the back of the eye. With RetinaSense-AI, you get quick, smart insights without needing to understand complicated medical details.

## 🚀 How to Use RetinaSense-AI

Once the software is installed and running, you can start analyzing retinal images easily. Here’s a simple guide:

1. **Load Your Image:** Use the "Open Image" button to find and select a retinal fundus image saved on your computer.
2. **Start Analysis:** Click the “Analyze” button. The software will process the image automatically.
3. **View Results:** After a moment, RetinaSense-AI will display the results. This includes any detected signs of eye diseases such as diabetic retinopathy or glaucoma.
4. **Save or Print Report:** You can save the report for future reference or print it to share with your eye doctor.

The interface uses clear labels and buttons to keep things straightforward.

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

- Use clear, well-focused fundus images for accurate analysis.
- Avoid blurry or low-resolution images.
- Ensure the image file format is supported (JPEG, PNG, BMP).
- Run the analysis in a quiet, stable environment to avoid computer interruptions.
- Contact an eye doctor for a full diagnosis or if you have questions about the results.
